About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow in SunLab at Georgia Institute of Technology work with Professor Jimeng Sun . Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in School of Electronics Engineering and Computer Sciences at Peking University (PKU) under the supervision of Professor Hongyan Li in 2019, and B.S. degree in School of Science at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2014.

My research interests are machine learning and data mining for healthcare, especially deep learning methods on Electronic Health Records (EHR) and biomedical signals like Electrocardiogram (ECG) and Electroencephalogram (EEG).
